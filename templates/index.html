✦ Features

Everything you need to shop greener

GreenSight gives you instant, actionable sustainability insights — no expertise required.

📊

Sustainability Score

A clear 0–100 score tells you exactly how eco-friendly a product is at a glance, with color-coded ratings for instant comprehension.

🌍

Carbon Impact

See estimated CO₂ saved or emitted, water usage, and air quality impact. Real numbers, not vague buzzwords.

♻️

Smart Alternatives

Get AI-recommended sustainable swaps tailored to the specific product you scanned, so you can make a better choice immediately.

🔬

Material Analysis

Understand what a product is likely made of and how those materials affect the environment throughout their lifecycle.

💡

Eco Tips

Personalized sustainability tips based on the actual product — from how to use it greener to how to dispose of it responsibly.

📜

Scan History

Every analysis is saved locally so you can track your awareness over time, revisit past scans, and compare products.
